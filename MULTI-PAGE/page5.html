Meet Shoji Meguro: The Composer Behind Persona

Shoji Meguro is the mastermind behind the music of Persona, blending multiple genres to craft unique soundscapes for each game.

Achievements:

  • Composed music for all mainline Persona games.
  • Known for blending jazz, rock, and electronic music.
  • Awarded for excellence in game music composition.
  • Influenced modern JRPG soundtracks.
  • Collaborated with various artists and vocalists.
